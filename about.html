About VaultGoal

VaultGoal is a free, no-nonsense savings goal tracker built for everyday people who want a simple, honest answer to one question: "When will I reach my savings goal?"

What We Do

VaultGoal is a lightweight financial utility tool that helps you visualize your savings journey. Enter your goal, your current savings, and your monthly contribution — and we'll show you exactly when you'll get there, with milestone checkpoints along the way.

No sign-up required. No data collected. No complicated spreadsheets. Just clear, honest numbers calculated instantly in your browser.

Features

🎯
Goal Tracking
Set any savings target and track your progress with a live visual indicator.
📅
Timeline Calculator
See exactly how many months until you reach your goal, with a real target date.
📊
Milestone Markers
Track your 25%, 50%, 75%, and 100% milestones with projected dates for each.
💰
Interest Calculator
Factor in your savings account APY to see how compound interest accelerates your goal.
🖨️
Print Your Results
Print a clean summary of your savings plan to keep on your desk or fridge.
✉️
Email Your Plan
Forward your results to a partner, family member, or financial advisor instantly.

Who Is This For

VaultGoal is built for anyone working toward a financial goal — whether that's building an emergency fund, saving for a home down payment, planning a vacation, paying off debt, or simply putting money aside for a rainy day.

We believe financial planning tools should be free, simple, and accessible to everyone — not locked behind paywalls or complicated apps.
