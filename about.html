About VaultGoal

Built by P. Beal, a financial planning professional with 26 years in banking and financial planning, plus 19 years of direct small business ownership.

What Is VaultGoal?

VaultGoal is a free, no-nonsense savings goal tracker built for everyday people who want a simple, honest answer to one question: "When will I reach my savings goal?"

Enter your goal, your current savings, and your monthly contribution — and VaultGoal shows you exactly when you'll get there, with milestone checkpoints along the way. No sign-up required. No data collected. No complicated spreadsheets. Just clear, honest numbers calculated instantly in your browser.

Whether you're saving for a home down payment, an emergency fund, a vacation, or paying off debt, VaultGoal answers the most important question: how long will this actually take?

Why VaultGoal Exists

Most savings goal tools fall into two camps: oversimplified (just a basic calculator) or needlessly complex (tied to a whole suite of features you don't need). VaultGoal splits the difference.

The goal is simple: show people the truth about their savings timeline. Not in a spreadsheet. Not in a complicated app. Just clean numbers that answer a real question: "When will I get there?"

Calculator Features
  • Goal Tracking: Set any savings target and track your progress with a live visual indicator
  • Timeline Calculator: See exactly how many months until you reach your goal, with a real target date
  • Milestone Markers: Track your 25%, 50%, 75%, and 100% milestones with projected dates for each
  • Interest Calculator: Factor in your savings account APY to see how compound interest accelerates your goal
  • Print Your Results: Print a clean summary of your savings plan to keep on your desk or fridge
  • Email Your Plan: Forward your results to a partner, family member, or financial advisor instantly
  • Free, No Sign-Up: No email required. No data collected. Use it as many times as you need

Ready to see when you'll reach your goal? Use VaultGoal to calculate your exact savings timeline with milestone dates.
